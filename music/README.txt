Place your .mp3 files here.

Then open script.js and add entries to CONFIG.playlist, e.g.:

  { title: 'Our Song',  artist: 'Artist Name', src: 'music/our-song.mp3'  },
  { title: 'Perfect',   artist: 'Ed Sheeran',  src: 'music/perfect.mp3'   },

The player will automatically build the playlist and enable controls.
